Google Girl Hackathon 2025 Semifinalist

I cleared the Google coding challenge and got the amazing opportunity to present my idea in the semifinals. My project focused on how AI can help detect diseases like glaucoma — especially in the early stages when symptoms aren’t obvious. I used image processing to analyze eye scans, aiming to make early diagnosis more accessible and accurate.
